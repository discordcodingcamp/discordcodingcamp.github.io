About The Cabins

Cabins are basically teams, and participating in challenges will earn points for your cabin. (For more information about points, please check the pins in our #leaderboard channel.)

Seasonally/quarterly, the cabin with the most points will receive an exclusive "badge" (Discord role); top point earners for each season will also receive an exclusive badge for their efforts. (Other badges will be made available through specific challenges.)

You can pick your cabin after participating in your first challenge. Usually, Summer will scream about your challenge entry in the #challenge-chat channel and ask you to choose a cabin, but if you forget, just ping us in #contact-staff and let us know which cabin you'd like. (You are also welcome to remain unsorted, but you will be ineligible for point earning.)

Cabins are limited to 10 members, including the Cabin Leader. As the server grows, so too will the list of cabins. Cabin membership comes with private channels for chatting and hyping up your teammates.

You can change your cabin at any time and for any reason, but previously earned points will remain with your original cabin. To change your cabin, just let us know in #contact-staff or message any Camp Staff directly.
